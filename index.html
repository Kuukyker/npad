About

NPAD stands for National Poker Association of Discord, every season, teams of 3 people are facing each other in a league competition. Captains from each team decide who to send and 2 players carry their team's flag and try to take the win home. They play head's up poker meaning one person against another at a table. At the end of the regular season the best teams qualify in the playoffs. The one team that wins gains legendary status forever.
